# Privacy Policy for Disemb-Arc

**Last updated:** December 17, 2025

## Overview

Disemb-Arc is a browser extension that provides a customizable new tab page for organizing bookmarks. Your privacy is important to us, and this extension is designed to keep your data local and under your control.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All data created and managed by this extension remains entirely on your device.

## Data Storage

This extension stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- **Bookmark organization data**: Your spaces, folders, and bookmark arrangements
- **Preferences**: Display settings such as which space is currently expanded
- **Chrome bookmark IDs**: Mappings to sync with Chrome's native bookmarks

This data is stored in your browser's local storage and is never transmitted to any external server.

## Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose**: To save your bookmark organization and preferences locally
- **Data access**: Only data created by this extension

### Bookmarks Permission
- **Purpose**: To sync your organized bookmarks with Chrome's native bookmarks bar
- **Data access**: Creates and manages folders in your Bookmarks Bar that correspond to your spaces

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All data remains on your local device.

## Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by this extension:
1. Remove the extension from Chrome
2. Alternatively, clear the extension's storage via Chrome's settings

## Third-Party Services

This extension uses Google's Favicon service to display website icons for your bookmarks. This sends the domain name of bookmarked websites to Google's servers to retrieve the favicon image. No other data is transmitted.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in this document with an updated date.
